THE SECOND PROTOTYPE WORKS ON MASTER SLAVE PROTOCOL.HERE YOU CAN USE EITHER I2C OR SPI AS PER YOUR COMFORT.

I2C PROTOCOL
>BURN THE i2c_master IN THE MASTER MCU AND i2c_slave IN SLAVE MCU

SPI PROTOCOL
>BURN THE spi_master IN MASTER MCU AND spi_salve IN SLAVE MCU

MAKE THE HARDWARE CONNECTIONS FOR BOTH REFERING TO TUTORIALS ON THE NET.
